Made for 3D-print shops

Know your real profit on every order.

Your filament tracker knows how much plastic is left. SpoolPilot knows how much money you made — it syncs your Etsy sales, subtracts the seller fees and your true cost of goods, and shows the net profit and margin on each one.

01 · Profit engine

See your profit, not just your filament.

Connect your Etsy shop and SpoolPilot pulls in each order, then runs it through your own cost recipe — filament weight, electricity, packaging, and seller fees — to show true net profit and margin per sale. The bookkeeping spreadsheet goes away.

Etsy order sync COGS recipe Net profit & margin

02 · Shop-floor mobile

Run the shop from your phone.

A native iOS app built for someone moving between machines, not sitting at a desk. Scan a spool's barcode with the camera, log a print in a single tap, and keep your whole catalog — with live margins — in your pocket, with big high-contrast controls made for hands-on work.

Barcode scan One-tap logging Live margins

03 · Variant mapping

Etsy variations, handled.

Listings are rarely simple — a planter comes in six sizes and colors. SpoolPilot imports a listing with options as one clean row, then splits it into per-variant products with a single tap when you want size-level costs and margins. No clutter until you ask for it.
