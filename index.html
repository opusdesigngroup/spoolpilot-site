Built by an Etsy seller with 3,000+ sales

Know your real profit on every order.

Your filament tracker knows how much plastic is left. SpoolPilot knows how much money you made — it syncs your whole Etsy history, itemizes the exact fees Etsy charged on each order, subtracts your true cost of goods, and shows the net profit and margin on every sale.

01 · Profit engine

The exact fees Etsy charged. Itemized.

Connect your Etsy shop and SpoolPilot reads each order's real fees straight from your Etsy payment account — then breaks them down line by line: transaction fee, payment processing, listing fee, even Offsite Ads. Coupons and buyer-paid shipping handled, your own cost recipe subtracted — filament weight, electricity, packaging. What's left is true net profit per sale, built to line up with what Etsy actually deposits.

Real fees, from your ledger Itemized per order Net profit & margin

02 · Your whole history

Import every sale you've ever made.

One tap pulls your complete Etsy history — the last 90 days, 12 months, or all time — so Finances shows your real business, not just the weeks since you installed. Tap any month on the revenue chart and the full P&L follows: revenue, cost of materials, operating expenses, net profit and margin for exactly that month.

Full history import Tap a month to inspect it P&L that reconciles

03 · Shop-floor mobile

Count sealed spools. Skip the grams.

A native iOS app built for someone moving between machines, not sitting at a desk. Scan a spool's barcode to add it to the shelf — or take one off — with quick plus-minus counts made for restock day. SpoolPilot watches your sales pace and tells you how many weeks each filament will last, so you reorder before you run out instead of weighing spools.

Barcode scan, add or remove Sealed-spool counts Weeks-of-stock forecast

04 · Variant mapping

Etsy variations, handled — and kept in sync.

Listings are rarely simple — a stand comes in four colors. SpoolPilot imports a listing with options as one clean row, then splits it into per-variant products with a single tap when you want per-color costs and margins. Edit the listing on Etsy later — retitle it, reprice a variant, rename an option — and a pull-to-refresh brings your catalog along, without losing any history.

Import as one row Split into variants Listing edits sync back

05 · Inventory that thinks

Stock counts, inferred from your sales.

Products are made-to-order until you say otherwise — no nagging about counts you don't keep. Track stock on the items you do shelve and SpoolPilot flags what's running low at your actual sales pace and queues what to print next. Sell kits? A bundle pulls from pre-assembled stock first, then assembles itself from parts — with the combined cost itemized live.
